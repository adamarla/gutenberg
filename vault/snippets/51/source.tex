\begin{snippet}
    \correct
    
    The tangent to $f(x) = \log x$ at $x=\frac{1}{3}$ is parallel to the line 
    $3x - y = 5$ 
    
    \reason
    
    The tangent and the line will be parallel when 
    
    \begin{align}
	f'(x) &= \text{Slope of }\left(3x - y = 5  \right) \\
	\frac{1}{x} &= 3 \implies x = \frac{1}{3}
\end{align}

\end{snippet}
